Fees

Therapy is an investment in yourself and your wellbeing. My fees are:

  • Initial consultation (20 minutes): Free
  • Individual counselling session (50 minutes): £50

I offer a limited number of concessions for students and those on a low income – please enquire if this applies to you.

Sessions are usually weekly at an agreed time. Payment is required 24 hours in advance. Cancellations with less than 24 hours’ notice may be charged at the full session fee.
