Fees

Therapy is an investment in yourself and your wellbeing. My fees are:

  • Initial consultation (20 minutes): Free
  • Individual counselling session (50 minutes): £50
  • Student rate: £25 per session

I offer concessionary rates for students.

Please get in touch if you would like to enquire about students rates.

Sessions are usually weekly at an agreed time, although we can discuss alternative frequency or longer session times. Payment is required 24 hours in advance. Cancellations with less than 24 hours’ notice may be charged at the full session fee.
