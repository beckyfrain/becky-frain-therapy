Why Choose Rebecca?

As a qualified counsellor and registered member of the British Association for Counselling & Psychotherapy (BACP), I adhere to a ethical framework that puts your wellbeing, autonomy and confidentiality first. I am trained in Person Centered Therapy. An approach that places you at the heart of counselling and offers a therapeutic space without judgement. The founder of Person Centered Therapy, Carl Rogers states: "We listen, but very rarely do we listen with real understanding, true empathy. Yet listening of this very special kind is one of the most potent forces for change that I know". My aim is to create a space in your life for you to be really heard. warm, empathetic approach creates a welcoming atmosphere. where you can feel truly heard.

Benefits of Counselling

  • Provides a safe, confidential space to talk openly and be heard without judgment.
  • Tailors therapeutic approaches to your unique situation for personalised support.
  • Helps you gain self‑awareness and move from awareness to acceptance and positive change.
  • Supports emotional wellbeing and mental health so you can live life to your full potential.
  • Can help with anxiety, depression, trauma, relationship problems, addictions, bereavement and low self‑esteem.
