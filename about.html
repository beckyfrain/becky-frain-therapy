About Becky

Hello, I’m Rebecca Frain, a professional counsellor based in Lymm, Cheshire. I am passionate about supporting individuals to navigate life’s difficulties and believe that everyone deserves a space to be heard and understood. My calm and welcoming approach helps clients feel safe, valued and empowered to explore their experiences.

I hold a Diploma in Counselling and am a member of the British Association for Counselling and Psychotherapy (BACP). This means I work in accordance with BACP’s ethical framework to ensure the highest standards of professional practice. I engage in regular supervision and ongoing professional development to continually enhance my skills and knowledge.

Over the years, I have worked with adults from diverse backgrounds who are dealing with issues such as anxiety, depression, grief, trauma, relationship difficulties and life transitions. I integrate elements from different therapeutic modalities to suit each individual, creating a personalised approach that respects your unique needs and goals.

Outside of my counselling work, I enjoy spending time in nature, practicing mindfulness and connecting with my local community.
